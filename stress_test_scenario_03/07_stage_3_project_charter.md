## 3. Commitment summary

The organization is authorizing a controlled custom app build to replace manual status reconciliation with an auditable availability control view.

Approved scope summary:

- itinerary status visibility
- leave status visibility
- office-expected status visibility
- role-based update and approval rules
- audit trail and history
- supportable ownership after go-live

Budget status:

- sponsor commitment: yes, in principle
- budget status: provisional funding envelope endorsed for definition and mobilization
- final implementation release: subject to confirmation of the Stage 3 conditions

Key constraints and high-level risks:

- multiple authoritative sources must be respected
- auditability is a hard requirement
- support ownership must be explicit
- the app must not become an uncontrolled second system of record

| Condition | Owner |

- operational handover must be shown before closure
