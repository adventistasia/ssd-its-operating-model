# SSD Staff Itinerary Status Control Scenario

Status: working draft  
Purpose: preserve the original request context used for this simulation exercise.

## 1. Exercise intent

This folder simulates how a Delivery Owner / Project Manager used the repository's governed [work_delivery](../work_delivery) framework in real time for a request that started as a simple itinerary tracker and then exposed custom-build level control needs.

The exercise is meant to surface whether the framework makes the next step clear, whether the work stays small enough for a light path, whether ownership and acceptance can actually be named, and whether the framework supports a custom app build when governance pressures justify it.

## 2. Original request

Request title: Create a staff itinerary tracker for SSD.

Requestor: Jane, Administrative Assistant / Secretary.

Problem statement provided:

- office staff cannot easily tell whether a staff member is on an approved itinerary
- office staff cannot easily tell whether a staff member is on leave or vacation
- office staff cannot easily tell whether a staff member is expected to be in the office
- itinerary revisions are frequent enough that the current status becomes stale quickly
- multiple authoritative sources exist, but the precedence between them is not defined
- staff are spending time reconciling chats, emails, records, and informal updates

## 3. Requested business outcome

Jane wants one trusted place where office staff can check whether a staff member is:

- on an approved itinerary
- on leave or vacation
- expected to be in the office

The more realistic business outcome that emerged is a controlled status app that also applies role-based update rules, shows which record is authoritative for each status, preserves history, makes precedence visible, and supports auditability.

## 4. Personas used in the simulation

- Jane, Administrative Assistant / requestor
- Angie, Sponsor and budget holder
- Delivery Owner / Project Manager
- Programmer / Technical SME
- Committee Secretary, owner of the approved itinerary record
- HR Leave Records Owner, steward of leave status
- Office Operations Lead, likely Outcome Owner
- Internal Audit / Records Management reviewer
- Security / Privacy reviewer

## 5. Governing rule for this exercise

The Delivery Owner must not assume the lifecycle in advance. The Delivery Owner must use the framework as written and then decide what stage the work appears to be in, whether it should proceed, whether it is small enough for a Work Brief, and which domains and artifacts are actually needed.

## 6. Important simulation note

Nothing in this folder is an actual approval, authorization, or acceptance record for live work.

Where decisions are shown, they are simulated scenario outputs used to test the framework's usability.
