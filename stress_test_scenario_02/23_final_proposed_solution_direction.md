# Final Proposed Solution Direction

Status: working draft
Purpose: state the solution direction the Delivery Owner settled on after the Stage 1 assessment.

## 1. Direction

Use a controlled visibility layer over authoritative HR leave records and formal itinerary approval records.

## 2. What this means

- keep HR as the owner of leave status
- keep the Committee Secretary as the owner of itinerary approval status
- present one trusted office view for staff availability
- allow revision-aware status display
- avoid duplicate encoding and avoid turning the tracker into a second system of record

## 3. Why this direction won

- it is useful to office staff
- it respects the source owners
- it can be supported without heavy custom process redesign
- it is small enough to stay bounded, but not so small that a Work Brief would safely hold the control story

## 4. What is intentionally not being built

- a new travel approval workflow
- a leave-management workflow
- an attendance system
- a shadow record that replaces HR or committee source records
