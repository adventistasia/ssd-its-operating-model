- no statement yet on whether the work can stay at Work Brief level

## 3. Early interpretation notes

### 3.1. What the framework made clear quickly

- If the current stage is unclear, start at Stage 1.
- Small work is allowed to use a lighter control set.
- The framework expects explicit ownership and acceptance even for small governed work.
- A [Work Brief](../work_delivery/work_brief/work_brief_specification.md) is only appropriate if one controlled record can still hold the whole control story.

### 3.2. What was not clear immediately

- Whether the request is still small enough for a Work Brief once formal itinerary approval and HR leave ownership are made visible.
- How to handle split authority between office usefulness and official-record correctness.
- Whether a single visibility layer can stay simple without becoming a second system of record.
- How many downstream artifacts are needed before the work becomes too heavy for the original request.

## 4. Working hypothesis after first framework pass

Initial view:

- The work looks small to the requester.
- The work looks medium-complexity to the Delivery Owner because the control surface crosses office coordination, HR leave ownership, records authority, and technical support.
- The safe next step is Stage 1 assessment, then a decision on whether definition should use a Work Brief or escalate to an Initiative Definition Document.
