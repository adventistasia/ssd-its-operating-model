# Additional Stakeholders Discovered

Status: working draft  
Purpose: make the emerging stakeholder set visible instead of leaving it implicit.

## 1. Why this note exists

The request started as a simple staff-visibility ask, but the framework made it clear that several hidden owners and reviewers matter to the control model.

These stakeholders were discovered during assessment and conversation, not assumed at intake.

## 2. Stakeholder note

| Persona | Role | Interests | Concerns | Influence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Office Operations lead | Likely outcome owner | faster staff coordination, less confusion, proportionate control | does not want a heavy project or extra administration | high |
| Committee Secretary | Approved itinerary record owner | approved itinerary correctness, revision traceability, record discipline | does not want informal chat approvals displayed as official truth | high |
| Leave-record owner / HR | Leave status owner | leave record integrity, privacy, and correct interpretation | does not want the tracker to become a second leave system | high |
| ITS / Microsoft 365 support owner | platform support owner | supportable configuration, predictable maintenance, no shadow IT | does not want an unsupported custom app hidden inside a small request | medium |
| Records / privacy reviewer | control reviewer | minimum necessary disclosure, data handling discipline | does not want staff status exposed more broadly than needed | medium |
| Disbursing Accountant | downstream consumer | timely awareness for travel-related follow-up | does not want to hunt through email for current status | medium |

## 3. What each stakeholder changed

- the Office Operations lead makes the work governable as a business improvement
- the Committee Secretary makes itinerary correctness non-negotiable
- the leave-record owner prevents duplicate authority over leave status
- ITS support keeps the solution from becoming an unmanaged custom tool
- records / privacy review keeps the tracker from overexposing personal status
- finance reinforces that usefulness still matters even if the solution stays light

## 4. Practical implication

The work can stay light, but it cannot stay ownerless.

These stakeholders are the reason the Delivery Owner treats the request as a small governed item rather than a casual administrative task.
