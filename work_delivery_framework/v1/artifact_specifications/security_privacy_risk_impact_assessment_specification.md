# Security/Privacy Risk Impact Assessment Specification

## 1. Purpose

The `Security/Privacy Risk Impact Assessment` captures the security, privacy, and compliance implications of the initiative so required controls and approvals are explicit before delivery proceeds.

## 2. When Required

Required when the initiative has security or compliance impact.

## 3. Required By

Gate 4: `Specification Complete`

## 4. Required Contents

1. data types and sensitivity where applicable
2. key risks and threat considerations
3. required controls and mitigations
4. compliance, privacy, and approval considerations where applicable

## 5. Completeness Rules

The assessment is complete when:

1. material security and privacy risks are visible enough to guide scope, design, and approval decisions
2. required approvals or control obligations are explicit
3. the mitigations align with the proposed solution and access model

## 6. Common Failure Conditions

1. material exposure is left implicit
2. required controls or approvals are missing, unclear, or disconnected from the solution
