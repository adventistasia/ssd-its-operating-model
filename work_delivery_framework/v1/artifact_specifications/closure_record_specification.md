# Closure Record Specification

## 1. Purpose

The `Closure Record` is the formal closure artifact that records the final closure decision, closure evidence, remaining obligations, and rationale for closing the initiative.

## 2. When Required

Always required.

## 3. Required By

Gate 8: `Closure Complete`

## 4. Required Contents

1. closure decision and date
2. closure owner
3. final acceptance reference
4. transition completion reference
5. outcome summary against intended objectives
6. final decision and open-item disposition summary
7. closure rationale
8. post-closure actions or obligations if any

## 5. Completeness Rules

The record is complete when:

1. the basis for closure is explicit
2. closure evidence links to acceptance, transition, and final control records
3. any remaining obligations are visible and attributable

## 6. Common Failure Conditions

1. closure is declared without linkage to acceptance or transition
2. the rationale is too thin to support later review or audit
