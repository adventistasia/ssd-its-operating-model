# Project Charter (Approved) Specification

## 1. Purpose

The `Project Charter (Approved)` records the approved purpose, scope, governance, and resource commitment for initiatives that require explicit authorization.

## 2. When Required

Required only when authorization is required.

## 3. Required By

Gate 3: `Authorized`

## 4. Required Contents

1. project purpose and scope summary
2. sponsor and authorization statement
3. budget or resource commitment where applicable
4. governance and decision rights, including named Gate Decision Owners
5. high-level risks and constraints

## 5. Completeness Rules

The charter is complete when:

1. authorization is explicit and attributable
2. resource commitment is visible where relevant
3. the team can act on the authorization without reopening its meaning

## 6. Common Failure Conditions

1. authorization is implied but not recorded
2. scope or authority remains too vague to act on
